## for fluent-bit

** Before applying the helm, change the namespace in fluentbit.yml accordingly

helm repo add fluent https://fluent.github.io/helm-charts
helm repo update

helm upgrade --install fluentbit fluent/fluent-bit \
  --namespace opentelemetry \
  -f spoke/logs/fluentbit.yml

Then in OpenShift:
oc adm policy add-scc-to-user privileged -z fluent-bit
